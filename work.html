04   Smorgasburg
Identity, Design
Parsons School of Design, 2021
A new generative visual identity system for Smorgasburg, the largest weekly open-air food market in America. The system is made up of a set of elements that are inclusive of all of Smorgasburg's diverse and vibrant food of ferings. When arranged in various configurations, the elements form a flexible, dynamic, and graphic identity.

06   Immense: Impact of Water
Typesetting, Design
2021
Immense is a quarterly science magazine publication that aims to capture the immensity of water and its impact on living forms. The Human Issue narrows the lens on the impact that water has on human life, existence, and society as a whole. By focusing on personal stories related to phenomena, contamination, drought, and warming, Immense hopes to both inspire change and stimulate meaningful and consequential discourse on significant worldly issues.

07   Work from Home
Design, Development
2021
A city-wide sticker campaign that adovcates for making Work From Home a permanent lifestyle with customized, connected, and interactive algorithmic design. The campaign quantifies how much one gains from working from home with six categories: no commute, more sleep, spending less money, no dress code, portable office, and flexible hours.
