Zebra Book Design
Parsons School of Design - Fall 2020
Adobe InDesign, Illustrator


I translated the Zebra Wikipedia page into a book publication. After analyzing the body of text and breaking it down into a systemized hierarchy, I used Adobe Indesign and Illustrator to create a design system for the content.
