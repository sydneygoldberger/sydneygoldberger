06   Immense: Impact of Water
Typesetting, Design
2021
Immense is a quarterly science magazine publication that aims to capture the immensity of water and its impact on living forms. The Human Issue narrows the lens on the impact that water has on human life, existence, and society as a whole. By focusing on personal stories related to phenomena, contamination, drought, and warming, Immense hopes to both inspire change and stimulate meaningful and consequential discourse on significant worldly issues.
