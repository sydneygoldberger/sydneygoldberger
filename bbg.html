01   Brooklyn Botanic Garden
Identity, Design, Development
Parsons School of Design, 2020
A new generative identity system was created for the Brooklyn Botanic Garden that captures the institution's commitment to regrowth in both the garden itself as well as within the community. Not only are the growing organisms evolving and changing over time, but the garden strives to progress and remain a relevant part of the community.
